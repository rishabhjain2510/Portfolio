Macromolecular Structures: Trends, Techniques, and Properties

Interactive Power BI dashboard showcasing comprehensive analysis of protein structures and molecular research data

Dashboard Overview

This comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzes macromolecular structures, experimental techniques, and molecular properties from a dataset of over 86,000 protein structures. The visualization provides insights into research trends, experimental methodology popularity, and molecular characteristics across different structural types and pH ranges.
